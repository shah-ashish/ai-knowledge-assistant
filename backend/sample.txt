1. REFUND POLICY
The company refund period for all SaaS subscriptions is 30 days from the initial purchase date. If a customer requests a refund within this 30-day window, a full refund will be processed to the original payment method. Requests made after 30 days are not eligible for a refund, but customers can cancel their subscription to prevent future renewals.
Exception: Subscriptions purchased during special promotional events (such as the Black Friday annual sale) are strictly non-refundable.

2. CUSTOMER SUPPORT HOURS
Our dedicated customer support team is available Monday through Friday, from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM Eastern Standard Time (EST). 
For support requests submitted during weekends or holidays, queries will be queued and addressed on the next business day in the order they were received. Urgent tickets can be flagged by selecting the "Urgent" option in the support portal, which are reviewed on weekends by the on-duty support manager.

3. REMOTE WORK GUIDELINES
All full-time employees are eligible for hybrid remote work. Employees are required to work from the physical office at least two days per week (specifically Tuesdays and Thursdays, which are core collaboration days). Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays are optional remote work days.
A home office equipment stipend of up to $500 is provided to all new hires to purchase ergonomic chairs, monitors, or keyboards.

4. DRESS CODE
The company maintains a "smart casual" dress code for all in-office work days. Employees are expected to dress professionally when client-facing meetings are scheduled, but otherwise comfortable and tidy attire is encouraged.
